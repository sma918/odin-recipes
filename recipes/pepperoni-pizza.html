Homemade Pepperoni Pizza

Descripton

A recipe from allrecipes.com that tells of a good way to make homemade pepperoni pizza.

It give direction on how to make the sauce and the crust and provides the ingredients to go on top of the pizza.

Ingredients

Contadina Quick Pizza Sauce

  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1/2 (12 oz) can of CONTADINA Tomato Paste
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil, crushed
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper

Fleiscmann's Easy Pizza Crust

  • 3 1/4 cups of all-purpose flour, or more as needed
  • 2 (0.25 oz) envelopes FLEISCHMANN'S Pizza Crust Yeast of RapidRise Yeast
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of salt
  • 1 1/3 cups of very warm water (120-130 Fahrenheit)
  • 1/3 cup of oil

Toppings

  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese, or more to taste
  • 1 (6 oz) package of HORMEL Pepperoni

Steps/Directions

  1. Preheat te oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit (220 Celcius). Grease two 12-inch pizza pans.
  2. Make sauce: Whisk together water, tomato paste, oregano, basil, garlic powder, onion powder, sugar, salt, and pepper in a mediumm bowl until smooth. Set aside.
  3. Make crust: Combine 2 cups flour, yeast, sugar, and salt in a large bowl. Add warm water and oil; mix until well blended, about 1 minute. Gradually add remaining flour, a little at a time, until a soft, sticky dough forms.
  4. Transfer dough to a floured surfaced; knead until dough is smooth and elastic, about 4 minutes. Add more flour as needed. (If using RapidRise yeast, let dough rest, covered, for 10 minutes.)
  5. Divide dough in half. Lightly flour your hands, then pat each piece of dough onto the prrepared pizza pans.
  6. Top dough with sauce, cheese, and pepperoni.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until crusts are browned and cheese is bubbly, 18 to 20 minutes. Rotate pizza pans between the top and bottom over racks halfway through baking.
  8. Now enjoy!
